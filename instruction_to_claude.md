How to synergise — the fix
The mental model should be a relay, not three parallel pitches:
Hero = the claim
Pattern = proof the claim is true (the diagnosis exists and is specific)
How it works = what happens after diagnosis — the part the reader can't infer yet
So "How the coaching works" should stop re-selling diagnosis and start answering the questions the first two sections raise but don't answer:
The pattern section shows you can find the habit. The unanswered question is: how do you actually fix a habit (not a topic)? That's genuinely new and belongs here.
What does a session/week actually look like?
How does the plan change over time as gaps close?
What's the proof it moves grades (evidence) + what's included + price.
